Indonesia International Book Fair (IIBF) 2022

04 - 08 September 2022 08:00 - 18:00

The oldest and largest book exhibition in the country, the 2022 Indonesia International Book Fair (IIBF) officially opened today. Held again at the Jakarta Convention Center (JCC), Central Jakarta, IIBF is a meeting place for publishers, writers and book readers. IIBF 2022 was attended by a total of 134 participants from within and outside the country with a target number of visitors of 25,000. The exhibition in Hall B of JCC and at the market place will start at the same time starting today. IIBF will be filled with discussions, book launches, author meetings, seminars or workshops for book and publishing industry players to the IKAPI Award which is given to writers, figures, and institutions who are dedicated in their fields.
